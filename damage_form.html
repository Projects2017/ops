Main Claims Database Page
Choose the Database which best fits your circumstance.  It is IMPORTANT that you use the CORRECT database to help us track and resolve the claims.  Once you pic the correct database, you can Display your claims or file a new one.

 
Display Database Page
This will show the user JUST your claims.  You can sort them by clicking the RED header.  You can sort by the timestamp that the claim was submitted/edited, the Vendor, the PO, the Item, and soon, the status oft he claim (NEW, at Vendor, a question for the dealer, at PMD for action, Credited, or a Replacement processed.)

Clicking on the "Magnifying Glass" will open the claim to view the details such as updated comments and specific credit and replacement information.

Filing a Claim
The new forms are very similar to the initial ones that we introduced in the past.  Each database file form is similar, but tailored to the circumstance.  The ONLY claim which a photo is MANDATORY is the case goods (furniture) database.  We can't process those claims without digital photos.
Damage-Claim (Furniture)

Damage-Bedding Claim
We've created a specific Damage/Warranty form for bedding.  If this is a damage claim, please indicate the PO and order date.  If a warranty claim, you can enter "00000" for the PO and put the estimated purchase date of the item.  Please indicate the bedding name, type (Mattresses/Boxs (Foundations/Sets), and size.

Parts Claim
Only use this claim if you are requesting parts which were shorted you on a shipment (table/chair legs, knobs, hinges, hardware to assemble pieces).  Do not file this claim if pieces were damaged.  If they are damaged, submit a DAMAGE claim which pictures.  Parts Claims are processed normally the same day at the Vendor and are sometimes shipped UPS or FedEx within a day or two.

Vendor Refused Claim
PLEASE ensure you either fax or email your scanned Bill of Lading which indicates that you refused the shipment in part of in whole when submitting this claim.  We need that BOL to issue you the credit.  When we receive the BOL, we issue the credit almost immediately.

Vendor Shortage Claim
PLEASE ensure you either fax or email your scanned Bill of Lading which indicates that you refused the shipment in part of in whole when submitting this claim.  We need that BOL to issue you the credit.  When we receive the BOL, we issue the credit almost immediately.
